Computer & Accounting

Duration: 3 Months

Daily: 1 Hour

Why Learn this Skill?

Learning computer and accounting skills in Nepal is highly beneficial due to the growing IT and financial sectors, which offer numerous job opportunities and career growth. Computer skills open doors to global remote work, entrepreneurship, and enhanced productivity, while accounting knowledge is crucial for financial management and transparency in businesses. Combining these skills provides versatility, a competitive edge, and the ability to leverage technology for informed decision-making and efficiency in various roles, contributing to personal and economic development.

Scope of computer & accounting.

The scope of computer and accounting skills in Nepal is extensive and promising. The growing IT sector and government initiatives like the Digital Nepal Framework create numerous opportunities for IT professionals. Accounting skills are in high demand due to economic development, with clear career paths and global recognition. Combining both skills enhances versatility, employability, and efficiency, providing a competitive edge in the job market and supporting entrepreneurial success.

Course Structure

Month 1: Photoshop

Week 1: Foundamentals Of Computer

  • Day 1 : Introduction to Computer.
  • Day 2 : Computer Basics Commands and Functions
  • Day 3 : Englis/Nepali Typing
  • Day 4 : Introduction To Operating System
  • Day 5 : Introduction To Software and Installation

Week 2: Miscrosoft Office

  • Day 1 - 3 : Miscrosoft Word
  • Day 4 - 6 : Miscrosoft Excel

Week 3: Miscrosoft Office

  • Day 1 - 4 : Miscrosoft Excel
  • Day 5 -6 : Miscrosoft Power point

Week 4: Adobe Photoshop

  • Day 1 : Introduction to animation: Creating GIFs and simple animations.
  • Day 2 : Final project: Applying all learned skills to complete a personal project or portfolio piece.
  • Day 3 - 6 : Review of course topics, practice exercises, and troubleshooting common issues.

Week 5: Review and Final Project

  • Day 1 - 3 : Work on final projects with instructor guidance and feedback.
  • Day 4 - 6 : Final presentations of projects, peer critiques, and discussion of next steps in Photoshop learning or professional development.

Month 2: Premiere Pro

Week 1: Foundation of Illustrator

  • Day 1 : Introduction to Adobe Premiere Pro
  • Day 2 : Importing Media and Project Organization
  • Day 3 : Basic Editing Techniques
  • Day 4 : Titles and Graphics
  • Day 5 : Audio Editing Basics

Week 2: Video Editing Techniques

  • Day 1 : Editing Techniques
  • Day 2 : Color Correction and Grading
  • Day 3 : Motion Graphics and Text Animation
  • Day 4 : Multi-camera Editing
  • Day 5 : Exporting and Delivery

Week 3:Project 1: Wedding Video Editing

  • Day 1 : Understanding the Wedding Video Editing Workflow
  • Day 2 : Importing and Organizing Wedding Footage
  • Day 3 : Editing the Wedding Video
  • Day 4 : Adding Effects and Transitions
  • Day 5 : Fine-tuning and Exporting

Week 4: Project 2: Ad Video Editing, Cinematic Video Editing, Podcast Editing, and Green Screen Removal

  • Day 1 : Ad Video Editing
  • Day 2 : Cinematic Video Editing
  • Day 3 : Podcast Editing
  • Day 4 : Green Screen Removal
  • Day 5 : Fine-tuning and Exporting

Month 3: After Effects

Week 1: Introduction to Adobe After Effects and Basic Animation

  • Day 1 : Introduction to Adobe After Effects
  • Day 2 : Workspace Overview and Project Setup
  • Day 3 : Importing Assets and Project Organization
  • Day 4 : Basic Animation Techniques (Keyframes, Transformations)
  • Day 5 : Working with Text and Typography

Week 2: Advanced Animation Techniques and Visual Effects

  • Day 1 Advanced Animation Techniques(Expressions, Puppet Tools).
  • Day 2 : Working with Masks and Shape Layers.
  • Day 3 : Visual Effects Basics (Compositing, Tracking)
  • Day 5 : Using Effects and Presets
  • Day 6 : Introduction to 3D Space and Camera Animation

Week 3: Motion Graphics and Special Effects**

  • Day 1 : Motion Graphics Design Principles
