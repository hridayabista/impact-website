Diploma in Graphics Desigining

Duration: 3 Months

Daily: 1 Hour

Why Learn this Skill?

Learning graphic design is essential in today's visually-driven world. It empowers individuals to effectively communicate ideas, explore creativity, and enhance professional skills. From branding to problem-solving, graphic design offers versatile applications across various industries and mediums. Whether you're a marketer, entrepreneur, or freelancer, mastering graphic design opens doors to new opportunities and enables you to create impactful visual experiences that resonate with audiences.

Scope of graphic design in Nepal.

Graphic design is booming in Nepal. With the rise of visual communication, there's a growing demand for designers in advertising, branding, and web design. As businesses focus on their online presence, skilled graphic designers are highly sought-after, making it a lucrative and rewarding profession to pursue.

Course Structure

Month 1: Photoshop

Week 1: Foundation of Photoshop

  • Day 1 : Introduction to Photoshop interface and basic navigation.
  • Day 2 : Understanding image resolution, color modes, and file formats.
  • Day 3 : Basic photo editing techniques: cropping, resizing, and straightening.
  • Day 4 : Introduction to layers: creating, deleting, and organizing layers.
  • Day 5 : Selection tools: Marquee, Lasso, and Magic Wand tools, and their applications.
  • Day 6 : Introduction to retouching: Healing Brush, Spot Healing Brush, and Patch Tool.

Week 2: Essential Editing Skills

  • Day 1 : Working with brushes and patterns for digital painting and texture creation.
  • Day 2 : Introduction to 3D modeling and texturing in Photoshop.
  • Day 3 : Combining images and text: Creating posters and digital compositions.
  • Day 4 : Advanced retouching techniques: Frequency Separation, Dodge and Burn.
  • Day 5 : Creating photo montages and digital collages.
  • Day 6 : Designing for web and mobile: Creating banners, buttons, and icons.

Week 3: Design Principles and Techniques

  • Day 1 : Introduction to Photoshop interface and basic navigation.
  • Day 2 : Understanding image resolution, color modes, and file formats.
  • Day 3 : Basic photo editing techniques: cropping, resizing, and straightening.
  • Day 4 : Introduction to layers: creating, deleting, and organizing layers.
  • Day 5 : Selection tools: Marquee, Lasso, and Magic Wand tools, and their applications.
  • Day 6 : Introduction to retouching: Healing Brush, Spot Healing Brush, and Patch Tool.

Week 4: Advanced Topics and Projects

  • Day 1 : Introduction to animation: Creating GIFs and simple animations.
  • Day 2 : Final project: Applying all learned skills to complete a personal project or portfolio piece.
  • Day 3 - 6 : Review of course topics, practice exercises, and troubleshooting common issues.

Week 5: Review and Final Project

  • Day 1 - 3 : Work on final projects with instructor guidance and feedback.
  • Day 4 - 6 : Final presentations of projects, peer critiques, and discussion of next steps in Photoshop learning or professional development.

Month 2: Illustrator

Week 1: Foundation of Illustrator

  • Day 1 : Introduction to Illustrator interface and workspace.
  • Day 2 : Understanding vector graphics: Paths, anchor points, and Bezier curves.
  • Day 3 : Drawing basic shapes and editing them.
  • Day 4 : Working with the Pen tool: Creating and editing paths.
  • Day 5 : Understanding layers and their importance in Illustrator.
  • Day 6 : Revision and Introduction to Project.

Week 2: Essential Tools and Techniques

  • Day 1 : Introduction to the Type tool: Adding and formatting text.
  • Day 2 : Using the Shape Builder tool for shape manipulation.
  • Day 3 : Working with colors: Fill, stroke, and the Color panel.
  • Day 4 : Introduction to the Gradient tool: Applying gradients to shapes and text.
  • Day 5 : Working with brushes: Calligraphic, Scatter, and Art brushes.
  • Day 6 : Introduction to the Appearance panel for styling and effects.

Week 3: Advanced Drawing and Illustration Techniques

  • Day 1 : Drawing with the Pencil and Blob Brush tools.
  • Day 2 : Creating complex shapes with Pathfinder operations.
  • Day 3 : Using the Mesh tool for realistic shading and coloring.
  • Day 4 : Working with the Gradient Mesh tool for advanced shading.
  • Day 5 : Introduction to the Perspective Grid tool.
  • Day 6 : Creating 3D objects with the Extrude & Bevel effect.
  • Day 7 : Using the Blend tool for creating smooth transitions and effects.

Week 4: Designing Projects and Applications

  • Day 1 : Designing logos and branding elements.
  • Day 2 : Designing icons and infographics.
  • Day 3 : Digital painting and illustration techniques.
  • Day 4 : Creating vector portraits and character illustrations.
  • Day 5 : Designing posters and flyers.
  • Day 6 : Final project: Applying all learned skills to create a personal artwork or design.

Week 5: Review and Portfolio Development

  • Day 1 - 3 : Review of course topics, practice exercises, and troubleshooting common issues.
  • Day 4 - 6 : Work on final projects, portfolio curation, and preparation for showcasing skills.

Month 3: InDesign

Week 1: Getting Started with InDesign

  • Day 1 : Introduction to InDesign.
  • Day 2 : Working with Text and Images.
  • Day 3 : Layout Essentials.
  • Day 4 : Styling and Formatting.
  • Day 5 : Designing with Color and Shapes.

Week 2: Advanced Layout Techniques

  • Day 1 : Exporting and Printing.
  • Day 2 : Interactive Documents.
  • Day 3 - 4 : Project Day 1.
  • Day 5 - 6 : Project Day 2.

Week 3: Editing with Lightroom

  • Day 1 : Introduction to Lightroom.
  • Day 2 : Basic Photo Editing.
  • Day 3 : Creative Editing and Masking.
  • Day 4 : Importing Presets and Creating Presets.
  • Day 5 : Exporting and Sharing.

Week 4 - 5: Implementing All Techniques.

  • Working on Real World Project and Handling the Client Properly
