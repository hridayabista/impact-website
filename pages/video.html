Advance Video Editing

Duration: 3 Months

Daily: 1 Hour

Why Learn this Skill?

Video editing skills can be a game-changer for NEPAL. They help in creating engaging content for training, reporting, and promotions. Additionally, they enable the production of educational resources and enhance community engagement. Internally, they improve communication and decision-making. During emergencies, they aid in informing customers effectively. In essence, mastering video editing empowers NEPAL to communicate better, engage stakeholders, and ensure reliable electricity services.

Scope of video editing in Nepal.

Video editing in Nepal offers a broad scope of applications across various sectors. It facilitates communication, training, documentation, and promotion. In the power sector specifically, video editing can streamline training processes, enhance documentation of projects and operations, and improve public outreach through engaging content. Additionally, it plays a crucial role in crisis communication, ensuring timely dissemination of information during emergencies. Mastering video editing skills can significantly contribute to the efficiency and effectiveness of communication and operations within Nepal's power sector, ultimately benefiting both stakeholders and the general public.

Course Structure

Month 1: Photoshop

Week 1: Foundation of Photoshop

  • Day 1 : Introduction to Photoshop interface and basic navigation.
  • Day 2 : Understanding image resolution, color modes, and file formats.
  • Day 3 : Basic photo editing techniques: cropping, resizing, and straightening.
  • Day 4 : Introduction to layers: creating, deleting, and organizing layers.
  • Day 5 : Selection tools: Marquee, Lasso, and Magic Wand tools, and their applications.
  • Day 6 : Introduction to retouching: Healing Brush, Spot Healing Brush, and Patch Tool.

Week 2: Essential Editing Skills

  • Day 1 : Working with brushes and patterns for digital painting and texture creation.
  • Day 2 : Introduction to 3D modeling and texturing in Photoshop.
  • Day 3 : Combining images and text: Creating posters and digital compositions.
  • Day 4 : Advanced retouching techniques: Frequency Separation, Dodge and Burn.
  • Day 5 : Creating photo montages and digital collages.
  • Day 6 : Designing for web and mobile: Creating banners, buttons, and icons.

Week 3: Design Principles and Techniques

  • Day 1 : Introduction to Photoshop interface and basic navigation.
  • Day 2 : Understanding image resolution, color modes, and file formats.
  • Day 3 : Basic photo editing techniques: cropping, resizing, and straightening.
  • Day 4 : Introduction to layers: creating, deleting, and organizing layers.
  • Day 5 : Selection tools: Marquee, Lasso, and Magic Wand tools, and their applications.
  • Day 6 : Introduction to retouching: Healing Brush, Spot Healing Brush, and Patch Tool.

Week 4: Advanced Topics and Projects

  • Day 1 : Introduction to animation: Creating GIFs and simple animations.

  • Day 3 - 6 : Review of course topics, practice exercises, and troubleshooting common issues.

Week 5: Review and Final Project

  • Day 1 - 3 : Work on final projects with instructor guidance and feedback.
